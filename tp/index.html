Flip Cup Tournament

Get ready for battle.

This year, the BeerCamp competition will take form in a flip cup tournament.

Team registration is now complete.

Each member of the winning team will walk away with a Flip HD Video Camera.

Rules

  • Tournament will consist of 32 teams, single elimination.
  • Matches are best-of-3 games. First team to win 2 games wins the match.
  • Teams consist of five players. All teams must sign up with five players.
  • Players cups are to be filled with about 2 fluid ounces of a beverage. This liquid may be beer, water, soda, energy drink, etc.
  • Playing surface should be relatively clear of obstructions and objects. No ‘backstops’ may be used.
  • In the event that only 4 team players are present for a match, one player may flip twice.
  • Teams with less than 4 players present must forfeit the match.
  • Sportsmanship is a virtue. Please don't be a jerk.
